About Us

When a young civil engineer launched Sreenivas Constructions in the little town of Madanapalle In Andhra Pradesh, it consisted of one man with a work ethic of “work, and work hard”. But, as Thoreau once said, "Success usually comes to those who are too busy to be looking for it."

That is exactly what happened here. Twenty-two years later, Sreenivas Constructions now has operations spread across the states of Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Telangana and Karnataka!

Next


Sreenivas Constructions has its headquarters at Bengaluru – the perfect location for a company focused primarily on infrastructure development.

The founder, Sreenivas Bodipati, today has twenty five years of construction experience under his belt and the company is fast emerging at the top among companies handling infrastructure development and engineering construction.

Our Values

Our aim is always to ensure that we never compromise on quality and always work with integrity in the execution of all our infrastructure projects, while also making sure of timely delivery and the satisfaction of all stake holders.

Sreenivas Constructions has a team of qualified engineers and supervisors who make certain that the standards we cherish are maintained. A valuable network of sub- contractors and suppliers ensures that the same attention to detail is maintained from the beginning through the entire life of the project. They make sure that the finished project delivers on the promise of the start.

Our team is important to us and every individual down the line is encouraged to actively participate in the thought process behind each project. After all, our ultimate aim is a product that we may justifiably be proud of and service that fulfils every expectation of the client.
